Odd Phenomenon in an Abandoned City (1982)

THESE WORDS ARE BLIND.
Through your eyes they see.

-- Philomene Long


WE WILL NOT EVEN KNOW
What they will call us.

Where will these words go?
They have no memory.

-- Philomene Long

One stone can tell
